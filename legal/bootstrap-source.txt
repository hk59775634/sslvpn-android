Native libraries libopenconnect.so and libstoken.so, and the curl-bin helper,
are extracted at build time from the F-Droid build of
net.openconnect_vpn.android versionCode 1120 (APK:
https://f-droid.org/repo/net.openconnect_vpn.android_1120.apk).

OpenConnect / libopenconnect is LGPL-2.1 (see upstream COPYING.LGPL).
Corresponding source for that F-Droid build is published by the F-Droid
and upstream OpenConnect projects; you can rebuild matching binaries from
https://gitlab.com/openconnect/openconnect and the F-Droid metadata for
net.openconnect_vpn.android.

The Java binding org.infradead.libopenconnect.LibOpenConnect is taken from
the upstream OpenConnect tree under the same LGPL-2.1 terms.
